Brand mood for the Calories Counting App is **Soft & Playful (low-stakes, habit-forming)**. Chosen 2026-05-07.

- **Mood:** friendly nudge, no shame, gamified — celebrate small wins.
- **Tone:** light and conversational, emoji-adjacent without overdoing it, second-person, encouraging.
- **Colors:** pastel base (butter, blush, sky); rounded vibrant accents (coral, lavender, mint); soft shadows.
- **Reference vibe:** Yuka, Finch, Duolingo-ish.
- **Known tradeoff to watch:** risk of feeling juvenile to users who want "serious" tracking — keep numeric/data views legible and credible even within the playful shell.

**Why:** User picked this direction over clinical, cozy-foodie, and performance options. It best matches an MVP that wants approachability and habit formation over fitness-bro credibility.

**How to apply:** Use this mood to drive all visual/voice decisions going forward — color tokens, type, component shapes (rounded), illustrations, copy tone, microcopy, empty states, and celebratory moments (streaks, completions). Reject suggestions that drift clinical, dark/aggressive, or editorial-serif. When designing the Calculate tab specifically, keep data readable so the playful shell doesn't undermine trust in the numbers.
